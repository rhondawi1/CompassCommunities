About Compass Communities

Compass Communities builds people-centered places across Oregon. Our team brings development, construction, and community partnerships together so more residents can access quality housing and supportive amenities.

How we work

We collaborate with municipalities, nonprofit partners, and subcontractors to deliver projects that balance affordability, sustainability, and long-term stewardship. From early site analysis through ongoing operations, the same integrated team stays involved.

Have a site, project idea, or partnership in mind? Contact us or explore our current projects to learn more.
