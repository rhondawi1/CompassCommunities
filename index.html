Our Vision

What Compass Communities is here to do

Compass Communities is built around a simple idea: when residents, public agencies, and delivery partners share the same map, communities move faster toward stability and opportunity.

The platform brings together project information, affordable housing pathways, and public-facing process in one place so people don't have to hunt across half a dozen sites or offices to understand what's happening on their block.

  • Give residents a clear, trusted place to track projects and find housing options.
  • Help municipal partners coordinate studies, entitlements, and construction with fewer surprises.
  • Create a repeatable, transparent playbook that can travel from one community to the next.

Over time, Compass Communities aims to feel less like a “website” and more like shared civic infrastructure: a predictable front door for projects, homes, and public decisions.
